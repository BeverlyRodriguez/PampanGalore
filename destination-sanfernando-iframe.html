San Fernando City Pampanga

San Fernando, officially the City of San Fernando is a 1st class component city and capital of the province of Pampanga, Philippines. Popularly known as the "Christmas Capital of the Philippines", the city holds the annual Giant Lantern Festival every December where large parol are displayed in competition.
