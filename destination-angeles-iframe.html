Angeles City Pampanga

Angeles, officially the City of Angeles is a 1st class highly urbanized city in the province of Pampanga, where it is geographically situated but remains politically independent. The name Angeles is derived from the Spanish El Pueblo de los Angeles ("The Town of the Angels") in honour of its patron saints, Los Santos Angeles Custodios (Holy Guardian Angels)
