How to Get to Pampanga: Your Gateway to Culinary Delights and Cultural Wonders

Located in Central Luzon, Pampanga is a must-visit location for tourists looking for a genuine and savory experience. This travel guide will take you on a tour of Pampanga's finest tourist destinations, mouthwatering dining establishments, and cultural landmarks!

Let's start now!

How to get to Pampanga?

From Manila, Philippines

1.1 By Land

Private Vehicle

There are bus Take the NLEX and leave at Angeles City or San Fernando, Pampanga if you like the freedom of driving. From there, you may use the local roads to find your way to any location inside the province that you want to go.

1.2 By Land

Commuter Bus

There are bus companies that run routes with terminals in Cubao, Pasay, and other transportation hubs. To get to several locations in Pampanga, such Angeles City, San Fernando, and Clark, regular air-conditioned buses and upscale coaches are available.

1.3 By Land

Jeeps and Vans

For a more budget-friendly option, you can ride jeepneys or vans that travel the Manila-Pampanga route. These vans and jeepneys are present and available at several Metro Manila ports and transportation hubs.

1.4 By Flight

Clark international Airport

Located in Clark Freeport Zone, this airport facilitates both domestic and international flights. To travel within Pampanga after arriving at the airport, visitors have easy access using taxi, grab, or shuttle to get to many locations in Pampanga.

1.5 By Sea

Subic Bay Seaport

Aside from Manila, you can consider traveling via Subic Bay Seaport if you are coming from other regions or parts of the Philippines. From there, you may take a bus or hire a private car to go the about 70 kilometers to Pampanga.

Getting Around Pampanga

Once you've arrived in Pampanga, there are several transportation options to explore the province:

2. Tricycle

Transportation

Tricycles are a popular option when traveling shorter distances or while touring smaller cities in Pampanga. They are pulled by motorcycles and have a sidecar that can hold a few people.

3. Ride-Hailing App

Transportation

Taxis and ride-hailing apps like Grab are available in the city like San Fernando and Angeles. These transportation options offer a more convenient and private mode of getting around the province.

4. Car Rental

Transportation

In Pampanga, especially in popular tourist destinations like Angeles City and Clark, automobile rental options are accessible if you want the freedom of exploring at your own leisure.

1. Jeepneys

Transportation

Within Pampanga, jeepneys are the most widely used form of public transportation. They may transport you to different towns and cities around the region and have set itineraries that they follow.
