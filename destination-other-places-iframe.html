Pampanga is the first Spanish province in Luzon. La Pampanga was the name given to it by the Spaniards when they first encounter locals along the banks or “pampang” of the Pampanga River. Today, Pampanga is known as the Culinary Capital of the Philippines because most of the best Filipino cuisines are from here.
